# Japan travel by Prefecture

Here is a detailed 135-day travel itinerary across all 9 regions of Japan (~4.5 months), including major cities, onsen visits, iconic natural sites, and longer stays toward the end of the trip for a relaxed finish.

## Region 1: Hokkaido (Days 1–15)
**Bases:** Sapporo, Noboribetsu, Furano, Asahikawa
- Day 1: Sapporo – Arrival, hotel check-in, Susukino nightlife, Sapporo Beer Museum
- Day 2: Sapporo – Odori Park, Clock Tower, Hokkaido Shrine, shopping at Tanukikoji Arcade
- Day 3: Otaru – Canal district, glass workshops, Sankaku Market sushi tasting
- Day 4: Noboribetsu – Transit (~1.5 hrs), check-in at onsen ryokan
- Day 5: Noboribetsu – Relax in onsen, visit Jigokudani "Hell Valley"
- Day 6: Noboribetsu – Optional: Bear Park hike, nature trails, photography spots
- Day 7: Furano – Lavender fields (seasonal), Furano Cheese Factory, scenic drives
- Day 8: Furano – Farm Tomita, flower parks, cycling tour
- Day 9: Biei – Blue Pond, Patchwork Road cycling tour, Shikisai-no-oka
- Day 10: Asahikawa – Asahiyama Zoo, ramen village tasting
- Day 11: Asahikawa – Snow or nature excursions (depending on season)
- Day 12: Sapporo – Return, Susukino nightlife or shopping
- Day 13: Otaru – Day trip revisit, local museum visits, shopping
- Day 14: Sapporo – Moerenuma Park and Hokkaido Museum of Modern Art
- Day 15: Transit south via Shinkansen or domestic flight

## Region 2: Tohoku (Days 16–30)
**Bases:** Aomori, Hirosaki, Sendai
- Day 16: Aomori – Arrival, Nebuta Museum Wa-Rasse, sightseeing

- Day 19: Morioka – Morioka Castle ruins, handcrafts exploration
- Day 20: Sendai – Aoba Castle, Zuihoden Mausoleum
- Day 21: Matsushima – Temple visits and bay cruise
- Day 22: Sendai – Leisure / shopping / optional hot springs
- Day 23: Yamadera – Scenic hike and Risshaku-ji Temple
- Day 24: Hiraizumi – Chuson-ji and Motsu-ji Temples
- Day 25: Return to Sendai – Local ryokan stay
- Day 26: Day trip to Onagawa – Coastal recovery, seafood
- Day 27: Sendai – Regional markets and cuisine exploration
- Day 28: Aomori – Return north, relaxation day
- Day 29: Lake Towada and Oirase Stream – Nature hike
- Day 30: Rest day / optional spa or short excursions

- Day 39: Day trip: Kamakura – Great Buddha, temples, coastal walk
- Day 40: Tokyo – Optional freestyle shopping, nightlife
- Day 41: Tokyo – Disney Resort or Ghibli Museum (tickets required)
